European Renaissance Art

The Renaissance was a period which immediately followed the Middle Ages in Europe and it saw a great revival of so much intrigue in the classical learning of Ancient Greece and Rome. Many things occurred such as the development of different inventions, philosophy, literature, and especially art. Most of the paintings and sculptures that came with the Renaissance started in Italy. Some of the biggest Italian masters with art was Leonardo Da Vinci, Michelangelo, Raphael, and Donatello. In essence, Renaissance art tried to capture the experience of the individual and the elegance and mystery of the natural world
